Iam Astha the creative force behind the avant-garde fashion label IndiGlam . Hailing from the vibrant and culturally rich tapestry of India, my journey into the realm of fashion has been nothing short of a kaleidoscopic adventure.From the bustling streets of Delhi to the intricate craftsmanship of Jaipur, I draw inspiration from the myriad colors, textures, and traditions that define the diverse landscapes of my homeland. Fuelled by a passion for celebrating India's rich heritage while embracing contemporary aesthetics, I embarked on the exhilarating journey of founding IndiGlam.IndiGlam isn't just a brand; it's a testament to the fusion of traditional elegance with a modern, global sensibility. With a keen eye for detail and a commitment to quality, each piece in our collection is a masterpiece that tells a story of craftsmanship, innovation, and cultural amalgamation.Our designs are a celebration of the vivacity and dynamism of Indian fashion. From intricately embroidered sarees to contemporary fusion wear, every creation is a symphony of colors and textures.
